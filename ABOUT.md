# About

## AI-Powered Live Sales Rehearsal & Prospect Simulation

Proxima is a context-aware, real-time sales training agent that helps Business Development Representatives (BDRs) practice before live calls.

By combining multimodal AI, live voice interaction, training script integration, and knowledge graph reasoning, Proxima creates a digital twin of real prospects — allowing reps to rehearse discovery, positioning, objection handling, and closing in a fully interactive simulation.

Unlike traditional sales coaching tools that analyze calls after they happen, Proxima trains reps before they speak to real prospects.

---

## App Description

Proxima enables sales teams to upload:

- Prospect context (LinkedIn screenshots, company pages, CRM notes)
- Company-approved training scripts
- Product positioning guides
- Objection handling frameworks

Using multimodal AI, the system extracts structured insights from visual and textual inputs and builds a contextual prospect model. During a live session, the rep speaks naturally, and the AI responds in real time as a persona-aligned prospect.

The AI evaluates performance against the uploaded training script while dynamically adapting objections, tone, and difficulty level. Reps receive immediate coaching signals and post-session analytics that highlight missed discovery areas, weak objection handling, and script misalignment.

In addition, Proxima supports role-switching simulations where users can act as the prospect while the AI plays the BDR — enabling teams to test, refine, and optimize their own sales playbooks.

The result: structured, repeatable, measurable pre-call training and playbook evolution.

## Core Features

### 1. Context-Aware Prospect Simulation

- Upload screenshots and CRM data
- Extract role, industry, seniority, and likely KPIs
- Build a structured persona model
- Simulate realistic prospect behavior and objections

---

### 2. Training Script Integration

- Upload company-approved sales scripts
- Map discovery questions and objection responses
- Score conversations against official methodology
- Highlight missed script components in real time

---

### 3. Live Voice Interaction

- Real-time, interruptible audio conversation
- Natural back-and-forth dialogue
- Emotion and tone adaptation
- Dynamic objection escalation

---

### 4. Knowledge Graph–Backed Reasoning

- Structured representation of:
    - Prospect → KPIs → Pain Points
    - Company → Industry → Fit
    - Script → Questions → Objection Paths
- Ensures coherent, persona-consistent conversations
- Enables adaptive and contextual responses

---

### 5. Personality & Difficulty Adjustment

- Adjustable prospect personality modes:
    - Friendly / Curious
    - Skeptical
    - Distracted
    - Budget-Constrained
    - Hostile / Defensive
    - Analytical CFO Mode
- Control objection frequency and intensity
- Progressive difficulty scaling based on rep performance

---

### 6. Role Switching Mode (Playbook Optimizer)

- User can act as the prospect
- AI acts as the BDR using the uploaded script
- Test real-world objection scenarios against your own methodology
- Identify weak positioning, unclear value props, or fragile objection responses
- Generate suggested improvements to sales playbook
- Enables data-driven script refinement

---

### 7. Full-Funnel Call Rehearsal

Simulate complete call flows:

- Cold open
- Discovery
- Positioning
- Objection handling
- Closing and next steps

---

### 8. Real-Time Coaching & Analytics

- Script adherence scoring
- Objection detection and categorization
- Discovery coverage tracking
- Confidence and pacing feedback
- Competitive mention alerts
- Post-session performance heatmaps
- Improvement recommendations
